Dry Van

General Freight · Truckload

Dry van is the backbone of US freight. FreightPoint covers standard 53' enclosed trailers for general commodities, manufactured goods, packaged food, building materials, and more across all 48 contiguous states.

With access to a broad carrier network through Truckstop and DAT, we can source dry van capacity quickly — whether it's a planned lane or an urgent spot load.

53' standard enclosed trailers
Full truckload (FTL) coverage
Nationwide lane coverage
Spot and overflow capacity

Reefer

Temperature-Controlled · Refrigerated

Temperature-sensitive freight demands precision and dependability. FreightPoint's reefer coverage is built around California's produce industry and extends to refrigerated lanes nationwide — dairy, pharmaceuticals, floral, and perishable goods.

Based in Fresno, we have direct access to carriers that specialize in California produce outbound — one of the most active reefer corridors in the country.

Refrigerated and frozen freight
California produce & Central Valley outbound
Produce, dairy, pharma, floral
Temperature-range coordination
