Meet the FreightPoint team

A small, experienced team built on decades of transportation knowledge — each person dedicated to a specific part of your freight experience.

MS
Manraj Sidhu
President

Manraj Sidhu is the founder and President of FreightPoint Inc, bringing over 7 years of dedicated freight brokerage experience to the company. A proud graduate of California State University, Fresno — where he finished at the top of his class — Manraj combined his academic foundation with a commitment to mastering the transportation industry from the ground up.

His brokerage career spans shipper relations, carrier development, load management, and the operational systems that keep a modern brokerage running smoothly. Manraj launched FreightPoint with a clear mission: to build a brokerage where communication is never the problem and every partner can count on consistent, professional follow-through. He is the primary point of contact for all shipper inquiries and freight quote requests.

Mehtaab Chahal
"Tom"
Operations Manager

Known throughout the team and carrier network as Tom, Mehtaab Chahal serves as FreightPoint's Operations Manager — the engine behind day-to-day load execution. Tom entered the trucking industry in 2021 and quickly distinguished himself through a strong command of freight technology and operations systems.

Tom's expertise in transportation management software, load board platforms, and carrier communication tools has been instrumental in building FreightPoint's operational workflow. His tech-forward approach allows the brokerage to move faster, communicate more clearly, and manage loads with greater precision. Whether it's coordinating capacity, managing check-calls, or ensuring documentation is clean from pickup to delivery, Tom keeps every load on track.

HS
Happy Sidhu
Accounting & Onboarding

Happy Sidhu leads FreightPoint's Accounting and Carrier Onboarding department, bringing an extraordinary depth of experience that few in the industry can match. With over 20 years in the trucking industry — the majority of which was spent running his own transportation company — Happy has seen freight from every angle: as an owner, an operator, and now as a key member of a growing brokerage team.

His 5+ years on the brokerage side give him a precise understanding of carrier payment cycles, onboarding compliance, and the financial processes that keep both sides of the freight relationship healthy. Shippers and carriers alike benefit from his institutional knowledge and the professionalism he brings to every interaction.
