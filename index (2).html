About Us

Our Story

FreightPoint Inc is a newly established freight brokerage — but the team behind it brings over 20 years of hands-on transportation experience to every load we move. From managing high-volume lanes to building carrier relationships across the country, that experience is the backbone of everything we do.

Based in Fresno, California — one of the most active freight corridors in the nation — we launched FreightPoint to offer shippers, carriers, and broker partners something that's harder to find than it should be: a brokerage that communicates clearly, executes cleanly, and treats every load like it matters.

We're growing fast. As we expand our services and open our physical office doors, we're building the kind of brokerage that earns long-term relationships — not just one-time transactions.
