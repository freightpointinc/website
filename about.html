About Us

New company.
Decades of experience.

FreightPoint Inc is rooted in Fresno and the Central Valley — a community we've worked in, driven through, and built relationships across for over 20 years. We know this freight. We know these roads. And we know the people moving it.

Our Story

FreightPoint Inc is a newly established, FMCSA-licensed freight brokerage headquartered in Fresno, California — right at the center of one of the most active freight corridors in the entire country. The Central Valley moves an enormous volume of produce, refrigerated goods, and dry freight every single day, and our team has been part of that movement for over 20 years.

We didn't start this company from a textbook. We started it from experience — working directly with reefer carriers hauling produce out of the Valley, coordinating dry van loads across California and into the Southwest, and building the kind of local carrier relationships that only come from years of being present, consistent, and honest in this industry. We work with a strong network of local trucks and owner-operators in the Fresno area, many of whom we've known and worked alongside for years.

FreightPoint was founded because we saw what shippers and carriers really need: a brokerage that is straightforward, that communicates without excuses, and that makes the entire freight experience as simple as possible — for both sides of the load. No runaround. No surprises. Just honest freight brokerage done right.

We are actively growing — expanding our services, deepening our carrier network, and opening our physical office doors in Fresno to serve the community we've always been part of. As we scale, our standards don't change. Every load gets the same attention. Every partner gets the same respect.

Our Mission

Back it up with honesty.

Our mission is simple: to make the experience of working with a freight broker as easy as possible — for the trucker and for the customer. It's how we operate.
