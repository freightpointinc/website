FreightPoint Inc was built from the ground up — but the team behind it has spent over 20 years earning the knowledge, relationships, and discipline that make a great brokerage.

Our Story

FreightPoint Inc is a newly established, FMCSA-licensed freight brokerage headquartered in Fresno, California — one of the most active freight corridors in the United States. While the company is new, the experience powering it is anything but. Our team brings over 20 combined years in the transportation industry, spanning brokerage operations, carrier management, software systems, and logistics administration.

We launched FreightPoint because we saw a gap in the market: shippers and carriers who needed a brokerage that actually communicates — one that picks up the phone, follows through on its commitments, and treats every load like it matters. That is the standard we hold ourselves to every single day.

Fresno sits at the heart of California's Central Valley, making us ideally positioned to serve produce, temperature-sensitive, and general dry freight moving in and out of the state. We cover dry van and reefer lanes nationwide, with a particular depth of knowledge in California outbound corridors.

We are a company that is actively growing — expanding our services, deepening our carrier network, and opening our physical office doors in Fresno. As we scale, our commitment stays the same: dependable freight coverage, honest communication, and a team you can actually count on.
