What We Offer

Our Services

Every service we offer is backed by the same commitment — clear communication, dependable execution, and a team that follows through.

Spot Freight

Fast, reliable coverage for time-sensitive shipments. When you need a truck now, FreightPoint sources capacity quickly and coordinates every detail from pickup through delivery — keeping you informed the entire way.

Overflow Coverage

When your freight volume outpaces your regular carrier network, we step in. Whether it's a seasonal spike, a lane you don't normally run, or unexpected volume, FreightPoint provides reliable backup capacity on demand.

Broker-to-Broker Support

We work with fellow brokers as trusted partners. Bring us your overflow, hard-to-cover lanes, or last-minute capacity needs. We handle partner freight with the same professionalism and clear communication we give every customer.

Responsive Communication

We pick up the phone. We reply to emails. We send proactive updates before you have to ask. Responsive communication is not a feature at FreightPoint — it is the foundation of how we operate on every single load.

Documentation Support

Clean paperwork from start to finish. We handle BOL management, rate confirmations, check-calls, and proof of delivery follow-through so nothing falls through the cracks and your records stay organized.

California Outbound Lanes

Based in Fresno at the heart of California's Central Valley, we have deep knowledge of produce, refrigerated, and dry van outbound corridors. If it ships out of California, we know the lanes and the carriers that run them.

Local Expertise

20 years moving freight
through the Central Valley.

FreightPoint is not just another brokerage posting loads online. We are a Fresno-based team with deep roots in the local trucking community. For over 20 years our team has been working directly with reefer carriers, produce shippers, and dry van fleets throughout the Central Valley — building the kind of relationships and local knowledge that take years to develop.

We work with a strong network of local trucks and owner-operators in the area. When you work with FreightPoint, you are not just getting a broker — you are getting a team that knows this region, knows these carriers, and knows how to get your freight moved the right way.
